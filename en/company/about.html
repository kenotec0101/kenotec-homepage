KENOTEC is an environmental systems company integrating dust collection, ventilation and automation, providing a consistent framework from engineering and supply to installation, commissioning and maintenance.

Company Overview

KENOTEC’s core value lies in connecting dust collection equipment, ventilation equipment, monitoring devices and control systems into one operating structure for air-quality improvement. Through site-specific system engineering and general-purpose automation architecture, we deliver solutions that consider post-installation operation and maintenance.
